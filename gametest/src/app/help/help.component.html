⤜ Navigation ⤛

Rooms
ex: Rooms change with every 'direction' chosen. You click on directions to traverse your envirnment. This will progress the story. If you return to a room, will new items or stats, no one room will be the same (ex: having a key to a door or discovering knowledge from another character).
Events
Events get triggered by entering certain rooms. Some rooms and the frequencies of these events are related to your 'Karma'. Usually events and how you handle them reap benefits for your inventory or your stats, while others can bring trouble. Some events progress the story, affect karma, or even trigger fights. Contain 'Inspects' as well.

Inspects
Some rooms and events trigger and unlock 'Inspects'. These are for triggering events and picking up items. Inspects can sometimes have adverse affects but also reap rewards depending on what you have in your bag or what your stats are.

⤜ Combat ⤛

    Battle

  • A 'battle' is a type of event where you must fight a foe. This foe can sometimes carry rewards like objects, gold, or experience for you to level up. Be careful though; Do not dare go up against a foe with a higher level or strength than you,

    • Taunts

  • Taunts are meant to intimidate your enemy and gain certain in battle stat perks during battle They are based around strength, speed, and karma. Sometimes a Taunt can backfire and have an Adverse Affect on your stats. Be careful who you Taunt!

    Strikes

  • A 'strike' is when you inflict offensive damage on your opponent. Whether or not the attack is successful depends on whether your opponent blocked and your speed.

    • Blocks

  • Blocks are meant to shield you from damage. Whether are not they are successful depends on your speed versus your opponents

    Enemies

  • All enemies have unique attributes such as lvl, gold, speed, objects, taunts and dialogue. In order to defeat an enemy you must bring their HP down to 0. Enemies can have varied strength levels and damage levels from their equipped weapon. It is up to you to gauge whether or not fighting is worth it at that moment or if you needs to explore more and level up. Enemies can also hold or guard treasure.
